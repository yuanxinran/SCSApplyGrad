Identify Needs

How might we assist reviewers with ApplyGrad?

Through a variety of research methods, we summarized the three needs that reviewers have during the review process and that are not well-addressed by the current system. These needs provided us with promising directions to pursue later in the summer.

Need for Integration

We recommend improving ApplyGrad by fitting it better with the reviewer’s natural workflow, so that they do not need to rely on to external tools for help.

Throughout a reviewer’s journey, there are many obstacles they encounter when trying to get their reviews done. We summarized the common obstales in each step of the review in the following image:



However, some of the obstacles reviewers can be addressed using the current functionalities in ApplyGrad. For example, in the first step, reviewer can actually use ApplyGrad to sort applications by the people they are assigned to, thus finding the ones that they are assigned to.


By sorting the "Rcvd By" column, reviewers can see applications assigned to them aggregated together


But during our interviews，many reviewers expressed they were simply not aware of this functionality, similar for many other functionalities. In many cases, hierarchy of layout and filters in ApplyGrad are not clear, so reviewers cannot sort in the way that they want. This leads to our first insight:

ApplyGrad provides a lot of features,
but many reviewers don’t notice them.
