Conceptual Prototype  

To test the perceived user need for a better review system, we created a conceptual prototype of a highlighting and note-taking system using the Google Doc and the Wizard-of-Oz approach.

Conceptual Prototype

There will be three applications for each reviewer to read, each consisting of a personal statement and resume:

  • First Application (Baseline): The reviewer would think aloud as they go through the documents.
  • Second Application (Manual Highlighting): The reviewer will use the normal Google Document functions of highlighting and commenting in their review.
  • Third Application (Auto-Highlighting & Tagging): While the reviewer has been working on the first two applications, the team will take note of which pieces of information the reviewer deems most important and highlight analogous information in the third application.When the reviewer has completed their review of the first two applications, they will begin their review of the third application which has already been highlighted, which mimics the function of a potential ML solution.
